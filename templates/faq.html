FAQ

What is the goal of all this ?

I always wished there would be a site where I could store all my board games I play online. This site strives to achieve exactly that. At the moment only game of Hex is supported, but there are more to come soon (Go, Chess, Amazon). Any user can create variants and comment on existing games.

How do I navigate in the game ?

Application strives to make navigation around the game straightforward and painless.
click on empty field
plays a move
left/right
go to next/previous move in the active branch
1-9
follow a variant
up/down
go to next/previous "junction" (node that has more than one children) in the game record
At the moment new variants are saved only if they end up with a comment.
